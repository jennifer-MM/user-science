Content Experiment

Google Optimize

Posted by Jennifer Menard on June 7, 2020

This week I reviewed the analytic data collected on my three goals. Based on this information I decided to conducted an A/B test on my homepage to learn if the location and design of my "Font Resource" link has a large impact on the number of people to complete that goal.

My original goals are:

  1. Clicking "Font Resources" link on homepage (EVENT)
  2. Downloading one of the four fonts on the Font Resources page (EVENT)
  3. Visiting more than two pages during a visit (PAGE VIEWS)

What I learned about my goals--

I had a five goal completions and because my goals were successive actions, a person could have completed more than one goal. The page view is high for the number of visitors which may indicate that the site navigation is unclear or confusing.

screen capture, Google Analyics goal completion graph

Google Optimize

After learning Google Optimize, I thought it would be a valuable experiment to change the layout of my homepage, specifically change the location and color of the link to the "Font Resources" page. I am hoping to see an increase in visitors accessing the page.
